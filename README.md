Limitation:
The identity features (identity_race, identity_gender, etc.) may introduce unintended bias: comments mentioning identity groups risk being flagged as toxic. As the competition host notex: "Models predicted a high likelihood of toxicity for comments containing those identities (e.g. 'gay'), even when those comments were not actually toxic (such as 'I am a gay woman')" (cjadams et al., 2019). We nonetheless include these features because identity mentions are a genuine signal for targeted harassment and hate speech, and excluding them would reduce the model's ability to detect such content. For production use, bias auditing per identity subgroup would be required.

cjadams, Borkan, D., Sorensen, J., Dixon, L., Vasserman, L., & nithum. (2019). Jigsaw Unintended Bias in Toxicity Classification. Kaggle. https://kaggle.com/competitions/jigsaw-unintended-bias-in-toxicity-classification
